Drupal

I build websites using Drupal, a powerful open-source content management system (cms). Drupal enables the development of websites ranging from small personal blogs to large corporate or political sites, like whitehouse.gov.

Site building

Drupal enables complex websites to be created quickly and cost­effectively. I find the right balance between using appropriate existing modules and building custom modules when necessary in order to keep your site lean and maintainable.

Theming

When creating themes in Drupal, I aim to provide pixel perfect implementation of designs with clean markup and css. I make sure your design shines across all browsers and devices, while placing strong emphasis on performance.

Development

I provide front and back-end Drupal development services. In addition to writing efficient code, this also means working with many Drupal components such as Drupal API, forms API, theme layer, Drupal coding standards and more.
