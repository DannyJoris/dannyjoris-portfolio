Drupal

I build websites using Drupal. Drupal is a powerful open-source content management system (cms) which allows you to build websites ranging from small personal blogs to large corporate or political sites, like whitehouse.gov.

Site building

Drupal enables you to build complex websites very fast and cost-effectively. While it is important to know and use the right modules, it is also good to know when to write your own module to keep your site lean and maintainable.

Theming

When building themes in Drupal I aim to provide pixel perfect implementation of designs with clean markup and css. I make sure your design shines across all browsers and devices, while keeping a high priority on performance.

Development

I provide front and back-end Drupal development services. Besides writing efficient code this means working with many Drupal components such as the Drupal API, forms API, theme layer, Drupal coding standards and more.
